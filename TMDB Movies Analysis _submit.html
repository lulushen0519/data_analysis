Introduction¶

This data set contains information from 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb) from year 1960 to 2015. Some useful information are movie quality ratings, popularity ratings, movie genres, budgets and revenues. In this project, I will mainy discuss three attributes:

  1. Movies release in which monthes are relatively popular, high rating and gain high revenues?
  2. What kinds of properties are associated with movies that have high revenues?
  3. Which genres are more popular from year to year?

Then, I will compare the average adjusted revenues of these two groups. Similar methods are used in analyzing the relationship between rating scores and revenues, and the relationship between budgets and revenues.

Conclusions¶

This project maily dealed with three questions:

  1. Movies release in which monthes are relatively popular, high rating and gain high revenues?
  2. What kinds of properties are associated with movies that have high revenues?
  3. Which genres are more popular from year to year?

From all the analysis above, we can conclude that the adventure, fantasy and science fiction movies are more popular among the audience. Also, those movies release in June, November and December are relatively with high revenues, popularities and ratings. Further more, a higher popularity, rating score and a higher budget will generate a higher revenue. Thus, for the producers who want to gain more revenues, they can try filming adventure or science fiction movies. Also, they can invest more in movie qualities and try to meet the audience's taste.
